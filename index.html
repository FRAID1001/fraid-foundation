About Us

Based in Kahama, Tanzania, FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION is dedicated to supporting children and families affected by Down Syndrome through advocacy, education, and healthcare support.

Our Mission

To provide essential resources, reduce social stigma, and foster a supportive environment where every individual with Down Syndrome can thrive with dignity.

How We Work

Community Outreach: We conduct field visits to identify families in need and provide direct assistance.

Advocacy: We work to raise awareness in society to ensure children with Down Syndrome are treated with equality and respect.

Transparency: For our international partners, we provide comprehensive reports to ensure all resources directly impact the lives of our beneficiaries.
