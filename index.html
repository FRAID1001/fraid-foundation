About Us

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, schools, and communities to ensure every child receives love, dignity, care, and equal opportunity.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome nchini Tanzania. Tunafanya kazi kwa karibu na familia, watoa huduma za afya, shule, na jamii ili kuhakikisha kila mtoto anapata upendo, hadhi, matunzo, na fursa sawa.

Our Mission

Lengo Letu (Mission)

To improve the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through healthcare support, inclusive education, advocacy, family empowerment, and community awareness.

Kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa afya, elimu jumuishi, utetezi, uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa wa jamii.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu (Vision)

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, supported, and given the opportunity to live a healthy, meaningful life.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo na jumuishi ambapo kila mtoto mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, anasaidiwa, na anapewa nafasi ya kuishi maisha ya afya na yenye maana.
