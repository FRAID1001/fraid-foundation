Our Vision & Mission

Dira na Dhima Yetu

The Mission

To improve the quality of life for people with Down Syndrome in Kahama by providing access to healthcare, inclusive education, and socioeconomic empowerment.

Dhima Yetu

Kuboresha maisha ya watu wenye Down Syndrome Kahama kwa kutoa huduma za afya, elimu jumuishi, na uwezeshaji wa kiuchumi na kijamii.

The Vision

A society where individuals with Down Syndrome are fully integrated, respected, and given equal opportunities to reach their full potential.

Dira Yetu

Jamii ambapo watu wenye Down Syndrome wanathaminiwa, wanaheshimiwa, na kupewa fursa sawa za kufikia kilele cha uwezo wao.

Why Kahama? Our Impact Strategy

Kwanini Kahama? Mkakati Wetu

Kahama is a rapidly growing district, yet specialized services for genetic conditions remain scarce. FRAID Foundation is the first of its kind in the region to tackle Down Syndrome head-on. We are a lifeline for 50+ families who previously had no place to turn.

Kahama ni wilaya inayokua haraka, lakini huduma maalum za matatizo ya vinasaba bado ni haba sana. FRAID Foundation ni shirika la kwanza la aina yake katika mkoa huu kupambana na changamoto za Down Syndrome moja kwa moja. Sisi ni kimbilio la familia zaidi ya 50 ambazo hapo awali hazikuwa na pa kwenda.
