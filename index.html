Who We Are

Sisi ni Nani

FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION is a non-governmental organization based in Kahama District, Shinyanga Region, Tanzania. We were established to bridge the gap between children with Down Syndrome and the support they deserve. We believe in ability, not disability.

FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION ni shirika lisilo la kiserikali lenye makao makuu Wilaya ya Kahama, Mkoa wa Shinyanga, Tanzania. Tulianzishwa ili kuziba pengo kati ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na msaada wanaostahili. Tunaamini katika uwezo, si ulemavu.

2026 Strategic Goals

Mipango ya 2026

  • Medical Database: Registering 50+ children in Kahama to coordinate therapy.
  • Kumbukumbu za Tiba: Kusajili watoto 50+ ndani ya Kahama kwa ajili ya tiba.
  • Inclusive Education: Working with 5 local schools for better learning environments.
  • Elimu Jumuishi: Kushirikiana na shule 5 za Kahama kwa mazingira bora ya elimu.
  • Public Awareness: Reaching 10,000+ people via radio and community meetings.
  • Uelewa wa Jamii: Kufikia watu 10,000+ kupitia redio na mikutano ya hadhara.
