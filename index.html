The Context: Why We Serve

Muktadha: Kwanini Tunahudumu

In many parts of rural Tanzania, including Kahama, children with Down Syndrome are often hidden or denied education due to deep-seated cultural myths. We exist to replace fear with facts and neglect with love. Katika maeneo mengi ya vijijini Tanzania, ikiwemo Kahama, watoto wenye Down Syndrome mara nyingi hufichwa au kunyimwa elimu kutokana na imani potofu. Tupo hapa kubadili hofu kuwa ukweli na kutelekezwa kuwa upendo.
Healthcare for genetic conditions is a luxury for many families. FRAID Foundation bridges this gap by coordinating medical expertise and therapeutic resources directly to the community. Huduma za afya kwa matatizo ya vinasaba ni ghali kwa familia nyingi. FRAID Foundation inaziba pengo hili kwa kuratibu wataalamu wa afya na vifaa vya tiba moja kwa moja kwenye jamii.

Our Strategic Pillars

Nguzo Zetu za Kimkakati

I. Healthcare Navigation & Early Intervention

I. Usimamizi wa Afya na Tiba ya Mapema

We connect children with speech therapists, physical therapists, and cardiologists. Early intervention is the key to independent living.

Tunawaunganisha watoto na wataalamu wa lugha, mazoezi ya viungo, na madaktari wa moyo. Tiba ya mapema ndiyo siri ya maisha ya kujitegemea baadaye.

II. Inclusive Education Advocacy

II. Utetezi wa Elimu Jumuishi

We work with local primary and secondary schools in Shinyanga to ensure they have the training and resources to welcome students with Down Syndrome into regular classrooms.

Tunafanya kazi na shule za msingi na sekondari Shinyanga kuhakikisha zina mafunzo na vifaa vya kuwapokea wanafunzi wenye Down Syndrome kwenye madarasa ya kawaida.

III. Family Socioeconomic Empowerment

III. Uwezeshaji wa Kiuchumi kwa Familia

A strong child needs a strong family. We provide parents with micro-enterprise guidance and counseling to ensure a sustainable support system at home.

Mtoto imara anahitaji familia imara. Tunawapa wazazi mwongozo wa ujasiriamali mdogo na ushauri ili kuleta utulivu wa kiuchumi nyumbani.
