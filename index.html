Who We Are

FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION is a non-governmental organization based in Kahama, Tanzania. We are dedicated to providing a voice for the voiceless and a support system for families navigating the journey of Down Syndrome. Our work focuses on medical access, social integration, and legal advocacy.

2026 Strategic Goals

  • Medical Database: Creating a registry of 50+ children in Kahama to coordinate therapy.
  • Resource Center: Establishing a physical point for family counseling and support groups.
  • Education Partnership: Collaborating with 5 local schools for inclusive learning programs.
  • Public Awareness: Reaching 10,000+ people through community radio and town-hall meetings.
