About Us

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, schools, and communities to ensure every child receives love, dignity, care, and equal opportunity.

Our Mission

To improve the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through healthcare support, inclusive education, advocacy, family empowerment, and community awareness.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, supported, and given equal opportunity.

Our Programs

Healthcare Support

Medical referrals, therapy, medicines, and transport assistance.

Parent Training

Helping families care and support their children.

Education Inclusion

Supporting school access and equal learning opportunities.

Community Awareness

Reducing stigma through campaigns and outreach.
