About Our Foundation

Kuhusu Shirika Letu

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is deeply committed to improving the quality of life for children living with Down Syndrome across Tanzania. We believe that every child, regardless of their genetic makeup, deserves a life filled with love, dignity, proper medical care, and equal opportunities to thrive within their community. We work tirelessly to bridge the gap between these children and the essential services they require.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kikamilifu kuboresha hali ya maisha kwa watoto wanaoishi na Down Syndrome nchini Tanzania. Tunaamini kuwa kila mtoto, bila kujali hali yake ya kimaumbile, anastahili maisha yaliyojaa upendo, hadhi, matunzo bora ya matibabu, na fursa sawa za kufanikiwa katika jamii yao. Tunafanya kazi bila kuchoka kuziba pengo kati ya watoto hawa na huduma muhimu wanazohitaji.

Our Mission

Lengo Letu (Mission)

To transform the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through professional healthcare support, inclusive education advocacy, family empowerment programs, and extensive community awareness to eliminate stigma.

Kubadilisha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa kitalaamu wa afya, utetezi wa elimu jumuishi, programu za uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa mpana wa jamii ili kuondoa unyanyapaa.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu (Vision)

To build a compassionate, inclusive, and informed society where every individual with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, and fully supported to achieve their maximum potential and live a healthy life without discrimination.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo, jumuishi, na iliyoelimika ambapo kila mtu mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, na anasaidiwa kikamilifu kufikia uwezo wake wa juu na kuishi maisha ya afya bila ubaguzi.

How We Work

Jinsi Tunavyofanya Kazi

Our approach is holistic and community-based. We identify children and families in need through intensive community outreach and medical referrals. We facilitate access to specialized healthcare and therapy guidance. Furthermore, we fight deep-rooted stigma through national awareness campaigns while providing emotional support and professional counseling to parents and caregivers to ensure a supportive home environment.

Mfumo wetu ni shirikishi na unazingatia jamii. Tunawatambua watoto na familia zenye mahitaji kupitia uhamasishaji mkubwa wa jamii na marejeo ya matibabu. Tunarahisisha upatikanaji wa huduma za afya za kibingwa na miongozo ya tiba. Zaidi ya hayo, tunapambana na unyanyapaa uliokithiri kupitia kampeni za kitaifa za uelewa huku tukitoa msaada wa kisaikolojia na ushauri wa kitaalamu kwa wazazi na walezi ili kuhakikisha mazingira bora ya nyumbani.

Education & Awareness

Elimu na Uelewa

What is Down Syndrome? Down Syndrome ni nini?

Down Syndrome is a naturally occurring chromosomal arrangement that has always been a part of the human condition. It is caused by the presence of an extra copy of chromosome 21. It is a biological condition, not a disease or a curse.

Down Syndrome ni mpangilio wa asili wa kromozomu ambao umekuwa sehemu ya maisha ya mwanadamu siku zote. Inasababishwa na kuwepo kwa nakala ya ziada ya kromozomu ya 21. Ni hali ya kibaolojia, siyo ugonjwa wala laana.

Facts vs Myths Ukweli dhidi ya Imani Potofu

Myth: People with Down Syndrome cannot learn. Fact: With inclusive education and the right support systems, individuals with Down Syndrome can attend school, learn various skills, and contribute significantly to society.

Imani: Watu wenye Down Syndrome hawawezi kujifunza. Ukweli: Kupitia elimu jumuishi na mifumo sahihi ya msaada, watu wenye Down Syndrome wanaweza kwenda shule, kujifunza ujuzi mbalimbali, na kuchangia kwa kiasi kikubwa katika jamii.
