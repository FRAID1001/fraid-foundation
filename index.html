Creating a Brighter Future for People with Down Syndrome

Join us in our mission to empower, educate, and advocate for inclusion in Kahama, Tanzania.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To improve the quality of life for individuals with Down Syndrome and other disabilities through advocacy, education, and direct community support.

Vision

An inclusive society where individuals with disabilities are valued, respected, and provided with equal opportunities to thrive.

Advocacy & Impact

Down Syndrome Rights & Inclusion Advocacy: We advocate for the social inclusion and fundamental rights of individuals with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. Our goal is to dismantle stigma and ensure that every person, regardless of their genetic makeup, is treated with dignity. We work closely with local government authorities in Kahama to promote policies that guarantee access to healthcare, education, and equal opportunities.

Our Specialized Programs

Early Intervention & Healthcare Support

We provide families with essential guidance on managing Down Syndrome from birth, focusing on early medical check-ups and nutritional support to improve long-term health outcomes.

Inclusive Education Initiative

Working with local schools in Kahama to create inclusive learning environments, ensuring children with Down Syndrome have access to quality education and social integration.

Family Support & Counseling

Hosting monthly support groups for parents and caregivers to share experiences, reduce social isolation, and build a resilient community for families.

Donation Policy

FRAID Foundation is committed to transparency and accountability. All donations are used directly to fund our advocacy programs, healthcare support, and educational initiatives for people with disabilities in Kahama.

  • Transparency: Annual financial reports are available to all partners.
  • Direct Impact: 100% of project-specific donations go to the intended cause.
  • Accountability: We maintain a strict audit trail for every cent received.
