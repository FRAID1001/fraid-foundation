About Our Mission

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is a non-profit organization deeply committed to improving the quality of life for children living with Down Syndrome across Tanzania. We firmly believe that every child, regardless of their genetic condition, deserves a life defined by love, dignity, comprehensive medical care, and equal opportunities to thrive within their community. Our foundation works tirelessly to bridge the gap between vulnerable children and the essential life-saving services they require to flourish.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation ni shirika lisilo la kifaida lililojitolea kikamilifu kuboresha hali ya maisha kwa watoto wanaoishi na Down Syndrome kote nchini Tanzania. Tunaamini kwa dhati kuwa kila mtoto, bila kujali hali yake ya kibaolojia, anastahili maisha yenye upendo, hadhi, matunzo kamili ya matibabu, na fursa sawa za kufanikiwa katika jamii. Shirika letu linafanya kazi usiku na mchana kuziba pengo kati ya watoto wenye uhitaji na huduma muhimu za kuokoa maisha wanazohitaji ili kustawi.

Our Mission

Lengo (Mission)

To transform the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through professional healthcare support, inclusive education advocacy, family empowerment programs, and extensive community awareness to eliminate discrimination and stigma.

Kubadilisha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa kitaalamu wa afya, utetezi wa elimu jumuishi, programu za uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa mpana wa jamii ili kuondoa ubaguzi na unyanyapaa.

Our Vision

Maono (Vision)

To build a compassionate, inclusive, and informed society where every individual with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, and fully supported to achieve their maximum potential and live a healthy life without boundaries.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo, jumuishi, na iliyoelimika ambapo kila mtu mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, na anasaidiwa kikamilifu kufikia uwezo wake wa juu na kuishi maisha ya afya bila mipaka.

How We Work

Jinsi Tunavyofanya Kazi

Our operational approach is holistic and community-centered. We identify children and families in need through intensive grassroots outreach and professional medical referrals. Once identified, we facilitate access to specialized healthcare and essential therapy guidance. Furthermore, we actively fight deep-rooted social stigma through national awareness campaigns while providing consistent emotional support and professional counseling to parents and caregivers to ensure a loving and supportive home environment for every child.

Mfumo wetu wa utendaji kazi ni shirikishi na unazingatia mahitaji ya jamii. Tunawatambua watoto na familia zenye mahitaji kupitia uhamasishaji mkubwa wa ngazi ya chini na marejeo ya kitaalamu ya matibabu. Baada ya kuwatambua, tunarahisisha upatikanaji wa huduma za afya za kibingwa na miongozo muhimu ya tiba. Zaidi ya hayo, tunapambana kikamilifu na unyanyapaa uliokithiri kupitia kampeni za kitaifa za uelewa huku tukitoa msaada wa kisaikolojia wa kudumu na ushauri wa kitaalamu kwa wazazi na walezi ili kuhakikisha mazingira bora ya nyumbani kwa kila mtoto.

Education & Awareness

Elimu na Uelewa

What is Down Syndrome? Down Syndrome ni nini?

Down Syndrome is a naturally occurring chromosomal arrangement that has always been a part of the human condition, being universally present across racial, gender, or socio-economic lines. It is caused by the presence of all or part of a third copy of chromosome 21. It is a biological condition that requires understanding and care, not a disease or a curse.

Down Syndrome ni mpangilio wa asili wa kromozomu ambao umekuwa sehemu ya maisha ya mwanadamu tangu asili, ukipatikana duniani kote bila kujali rangi, jinsia, au hali ya kiuchumi. Inasababishwa na kuwepo kwa nakala ya tatu ya kromozomu ya 21. Ni hali ya kibaolojia inayohitaji uelewa na matunzo, siyo ugonjwa wala laana.

Debunking Myths Ukweli dhidi ya Imani Potofu

Common Myth: People with Down Syndrome cannot learn or be productive. The Fact: With inclusive education, early intervention, and the right support systems, individuals with Down Syndrome can attend mainstream schools, learn diverse vocational skills, and contribute significantly to their communities and the economy.

Imani Potofu: Watu wenye Down Syndrome hawawezi kujifunza au kuwa na tija. Ukweli: Kupitia elimu jumuishi, uingiliaji wa mapema, na mifumo sahihi ya msaada, watu wenye Down Syndrome wanaweza kwenda shule za kawaida, kujifunza ujuzi mbalimbali wa kazi, na kuchangia kwa kiasi kikubwa katika jamii na uchumi.
