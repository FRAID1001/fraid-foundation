About Our Vision

Based in Kahama, Tanzania, Fraid Foundation is a dedicated non-profit organization focused on uplifting marginalized communities through sustainable social programs and healthcare advocacy.

Our Mission

To provide essential resources and sustainable support to vulnerable children and families, fostering hope and self-reliance in our society.
