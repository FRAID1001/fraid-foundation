Family Stability & Social Challenges

Utulivu wa Familia na Changamoto za Jamii

Stopping Family Abandonment

We are tackling a serious crisis in Kahama where many fathers flee their families after the birth of a child with Down Syndrome. This leaves mothers to shoulder the medical and financial burden alone.

FRAID Foundation works to provide psychological counseling for fathers and creates a safety net for abandoned mothers to ensure every child is raised in a supportive environment.

Komesha Utelekezwaji wa Familia

Tunapambana na janga kubwa Kahama ambapo akina baba wengi hukimbia familia zao baada ya mtoto kuzaliwa na Down Syndrome. Hali hii inawaacha akina mama wakibeba mzigo wa matibabu na malezi peke yao.

FRAID Foundation inafanya kazi ya kutoa ushauri wa kisaikolojia kwa akina baba na kutengeneza mfumo wa usalama kwa akina mama walioachwa ili kuhakikisha kila mtoto anakua katika mazingira ya upendo.

Dira na Dhima (Vision & Mission)

Dira na Dhima

The Mission

To improve the quality of life for people with Down Syndrome in Kahama by providing healthcare, inclusive education, and socioeconomic empowerment for mothers.

Dhima Yetu

Kuboresha maisha ya watu wenye Down Syndrome Kahama kupitia huduma za afya, elimu jumuishi, na uwezeshaji wa kiuchumi kwa akina mama.

The Vision

A society where individuals with Down Syndrome are fully integrated, respected, and given equal opportunities within stable, loving families.

Dira Yetu

Jamii ambapo watu wenye Down Syndrome wanathaminiwa, wanaheshimiwa, na kupewa fursa sawa ndani ya familia imara zenye upendo.
