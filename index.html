About Us

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, schools, and communities to ensure every child receives love, dignity, care, and equal opportunity.

We believe that every child deserves the chance to grow, learn, and reach their full potential regardless of their condition.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome nchini Tanzania. Tunafanya kazi kwa karibu na familia, watoa huduma za afya, shule, na jamii ili kuhakikisha kila mtoto anapata upendo, hadhi, matunzo, na fursa sawa.

Tunaamini kwamba kila mtoto anastahili nafasi ya kukua, kujifunza, na kufikia uwezo wake kamili bila kujali hali yake.

Our Mission

Lengo Letu (Mission)

To improve the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through healthcare support, inclusive education, advocacy, family empowerment, and community awareness. We are committed to promoting dignity, equality, acceptance, and equal opportunities so that every child can grow and succeed.

Kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa afya, elimu jumuishi, utetezi, uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa wa jamii. Tumejitolea kukuza hadhi, usawa, kukubalika, na fursa sawa ili kila mtoto akue na kufanikiwa.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu (Vision)

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, supported, and given the opportunity to live a healthy, meaningful, and successful life without discrimination.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo na jumuishi ambapo kila mtoto mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, anasaidiwa, na anapewa nafasi ya kuishi maisha ya afya, yenye maana, na yenye mafanikio bila ubaguzi.

How We Work

Jinsi Tunavyofanya Kazi

At FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation, we identify children and families in need through community outreach, referrals, and direct engagement. We listen to their challenges and provide practical support with compassion and respect.

We help families access healthcare services, therapy, medicines, transport assistance, and early intervention programs. We also promote inclusive education by encouraging school enrollment and equal learning opportunities.

Through awareness campaigns, seminars, and community outreach, we fight stigma and misinformation while promoting acceptance and understanding.

We empower parents and caregivers through training, counseling, and emotional support so they can confidently care for their children.

We also welcome partnerships with donors, volunteers, educators, and healthcare professionals who wish to make a lasting impact.

Katika FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation, tunawatambua watoto na familia zenye mahitaji kupitia uhamasishaji wa jamii, marejeo, na ushirikiano wa moja kwa moja. Tunahikiliza changamoto zao na kutoa msaada wa vitendo kwa upendo na heshima.

Tunasaidia familia kupata huduma za afya, tiba, dawa, msaada wa usafiri, na programu za kuingilia kati mapema. Pia tunahimiza elimu jumuishi kwa kuhimiza uandikishaji shule na fursa sawa za kujifunza.

Kupitia kampeni za uelewa, semina, na uhamasishaji wa jamii, tunapambana na unyanyapaa na taarifa potofu huku tukikuza kukubalika na uelewa.

Tunawawezesha wazazi na walezi kupitia mafunzo, ushauri, na msaada wa kihisia ili waweze kuwatunza watoto wao kwa ujasiri.

Pia tunakaribisha ushirikiano na wafadhili, watu wa kujitolea, waelimishaji, na wataalamu wa afya wanaotaka kuleta mabadiliko ya kudumu.

Our Programs

Programu Zetu

Healthcare Support: Providing medical referrals, therapy guidance, medicines support, and transport assistance.

Parent Training: Helping parents understand how to care for and support children with Down Syndrome.

Education Inclusion: Promoting access to schools and equal learning opportunities.

Community Awareness: Reducing stigma through education campaigns, seminars, and outreach.

Msaada wa Afya: Kutoa marejeo ya matibabu, mwongozo wa tiba, msaada wa dawa, na usafiri.

Mafunzo ya Wazazi: Kusaidia wazazi kuelewa jinsi ya kutunza na kusaidia watoto wenye Down Syndrome.

Elimu Jumuishi: Kuhimiza upatikanaji wa shule na fursa sawa za kujifunza.

Uelewa wa Jamii: Kupunguza unyanyapaa kupitia kampeni za elimu, semina, na uhamasishaji.
