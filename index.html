About Us

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, schools, and communities to ensure every child receives love, dignity, care, and equal opportunity.

We believe that every child deserves the chance to grow, learn, and reach their full potential regardless of their condition.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome nchini Tanzania. Tunafanya kazi kwa karibu na familia, watoa huduma za afya, shule, na jamii ili kuhakikisha kila mtoto anapata upendo, hadhi, matunzo, na fursa sawa.

Tunaamini kwamba kila mtoto anastahili nafasi ya kukua, kujifunza, na kufikia uwezo wake kamili bila kujali hali yake.

Our Mission

Lengo Letu (Mission)

To improve the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through healthcare support, inclusive education, advocacy, family empowerment, and community awareness. We are committed to promoting dignity, equality, acceptance, and equal opportunities so that every child can grow and succeed.

Kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa afya, elimu jumuishi, utetezi, uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa wa jamii. Tumejitolea kukuza hadhi, usawa, kukubalika, na fursa sawa ili kila mtoto akue na kufanikiwa.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu (Vision)

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, supported, and given the opportunity to live a healthy, meaningful, and successful life without discrimination.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo na jumuishi ambapo kila mtoto mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, anasaidiwa, na anapewa nafasi ya kuishi maisha ya afya, yenye maana, na yenye mafanikio bila ubaguzi.

Education & Awareness

Elimu na Uelewa

What is Down Syndrome? Down Syndrome ni nini?

It is a genetic condition caused by an extra copy of chromosome 21. It is not a disease, but a biological condition that leads to developmental delays.

Ni hali ya kimaumbile inayotokea wakati mtoto anapozaliwa akiwa na nakala ya ziada ya kromozomu ya 21. Hii siyo ugonjwa, bali ni hali ya kibaolojia.

Facts vs Myths Ukweli vs Imani Potofu

Myth: It's a curse. Fact: It's biological and can happen to any family. Myth: They can't learn. Fact: They can learn with inclusive support.

Imani: Ni laana. Ukweli: Ni hali ya kibaolojia. Imani: Hawajifunzi. Ukweli: Wanaweza kujifunza wakipewa msaada sahihi mapema.

How We Work

Jinsi Tunavyofanya Kazi

At FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation, we identify children and families in need through community outreach, referrals, and direct engagement. We help families access healthcare, therapy, and inclusive education. Through awareness campaigns, we fight stigma while empowering parents through counseling and emotional support.

Katika FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation, tunawatambua watoto na familia zenye mahitaji kupitia uhamasishaji wa jamii na marejeo. Tunasaidia familia kupata huduma za afya, tiba, na elimu jumuishi. Kupitia kampeni za uelewa, tunapambana na unyanyapaa huku tukiwawezesha wazazi kupitia ushauri.
