About Us

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, schools, and communities to ensure every child receives love, dignity, care, and equal opportunity.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome nchini Tanzania. Tunafanya kazi kwa karibu na familia, watoa huduma za afya, shule, na jamii ili kuhakikisha kila mtoto anapata upendo, hadhi, na matunzo.

Our Mission

Lengo Letu (Mission)

To improve the lives of children with Down Syndrome and their families through healthcare support, inclusive education, advocacy, family empowerment, and community awareness.

Kuboresha maisha ya watoto wenye Down Syndrome na familia zao kupitia msaada wa afya, elimu jumuishi, utetezi, uwezeshaji wa familia, na uelewa wa jamii.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu (Vision)

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome is valued, respected, supported, and given the opportunity to live a healthy life without discrimination.

Kujenga jamii yenye upendo na jumuishi ambapo kila mtoto mwenye Down Syndrome anathaminiwa, anaheshimiwa, anasaidiwa, na anapewa nafasi ya kuishi maisha ya afya bila ubaguzi.

Education & Awareness

Elimu na Uelewa

What is Down Syndrome? Down Syndrome ni nini?

It is a genetic condition caused by an extra copy of chromosome 21. It is not a disease, but a biological condition.

Ni hali ya kimaumbile inayotokea wakati mtoto anapozaliwa akiwa na nakala ya ziada ya kromozomu ya 21. Hii siyo ugonjwa.

Facts vs Myths Ukweli vs Imani Potofu

Myth: It's a curse. Fact: It's biological and can happen to any family. They can learn with inclusive support.

Imani: Ni laana. Ukweli: Ni hali ya kibaolojia na inaweza kumtokea mzazi yeyote. Wanaweza kujifunza wakipewa msaada.

How We Work

Jinsi Tunavyofanya Kazi

We identify children and families in need through community outreach. We help them access healthcare, therapy, and inclusive education. We fight stigma while empowering parents through counseling.

Tunawatambua watoto na familia zenye mahitaji. Tunasaidia kupata huduma za afya, tiba, na elimu jumuishi. Tunapambana na unyanyapaa huku tukiwawezesha wazazi kupitia ushauri.
