About Us

We are dedicated to improving the lives of children with Down Syndrome through awareness, education, advocacy, and community support in Tanzania.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive society where every child with Down Syndrome can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.

Project Overview

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is a community-based initiative in Kahama District, Tanzania.

We support 150 children and families through healthcare, transport, medicines, awareness, training, and inclusion programs.
