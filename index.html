About Us

Kuhusu Sisi

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation is committed to improving the lives of children in Tanzania. We work closely with families, schools, and healthcare providers to ensure every child receives love, dignity, and care.

FRAID Down Syndrome Foundation imejitolea kuboresha maisha ya watoto nchini Tanzania. Tunafanya kazi na familia, shule, na watoa huduma za afya kuhakikisha kila mtoto anapata upendo, heshima, na malezi bora.

Social Challenge

Changamoto ya Kijamii

The Issue: In Kahama, many fathers abandon their families after a Down Syndrome diagnosis. We provide psychological support to keep families together.

Tatizo: Kahama, akina baba wengi hutelekeza familia baada ya mtoto kugundulika na Down Syndrome. Tunatoa ushauri wa kisaikolojia kuziweka familia pamoja.
