Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for individuals with Down Syndrome and other disabilities through advocacy, education, and direct community support in Kahama and beyond.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive society where individuals with disabilities are valued, respected, and provided with equal opportunities to thrive and reach their full potential.

Advocacy & Programs

Down Syndrome Rights & Inclusion Advocacy: We advocate for the social inclusion and fundamental rights of individuals with Down Syndrome in Tanzania. Our goal is to dismantle stigma and ensure that every person, regardless of their genetic makeup, is treated with dignity. We work closely with local government authorities in Kahama to promote policies that guarantee access to healthcare, education, and equal opportunities.

1. Early Intervention & Healthcare Support

We provide families with essential guidance on managing Down Syndrome from birth. This includes coordinating medical check-ups, monitoring cognitive development, and providing nutritional support to ensure a healthy start for children.

2. Inclusive Education Initiative

Our team works with local schools and teachers to create inclusive learning environments. We believe that children with Down Syndrome should not be hidden but should be integrated into the classroom to learn and socialize with their peers.

3. Family Support & Counseling

Raising a child with a disability can be challenging. We host monthly support groups in Kahama for parents and caregivers to share their experiences, reduce social isolation, and build a strong, resilient community.

Donation Policy & Transparency

FRAID Foundation is committed to the highest standards of transparency and accountability. Every donation we receive is handled with care to ensure it reaches the intended beneficiaries.

  • Transparency: Detailed annual financial reports are provided to our partners and donors.
  • Efficiency: Funds are prioritized for direct project implementation in the community.
  • Accountability: We maintain strict internal controls and documentation for all expenditures.
  • Donors' Rights: We respect the privacy of our donors and ensure their contributions are used as requested.
