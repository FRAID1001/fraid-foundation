Who We Are

Sisi ni Nani

FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION is based in Kahama, Tanzania. We provide a support system for families navigating Down Syndrome through medical access and advocacy.

FRAID DOWNSYNDROME FOUNDATION ni taasisi iliyopo Kahama, Tanzania. Tunatoa mfumo wa msaada kwa familia zenye watoto wa Down Syndrome kupitia huduma za afya na utetezi wa haki zao.

2026 Strategic Goals

Mipango Yetu 2026

  • Register 50+ children for specialized therapy.
  • Partner with 5 local schools for inclusive learning.
  • Reach 10,000+ people through awareness campaigns.
  • Kusajili watoto 50+ kwa ajili ya tiba maalum.
  • Kushirikiana na shule 5 za mtaani kwa ajili ya elimu jumuishi.
  • Kufikia watu 10,000+ kupitia kampeni za uelewa jamii.
